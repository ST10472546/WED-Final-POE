Our Programs

Robotics Program

Our robotics program teaches women how to design, build, and program robots. Students learn about sensors, actuators, microcontrollers, and computer vision while working on real-world projects.

Graduates have gone on to work at companies like Boston Dynamics, Tesla, and NASA.

Web Development Bootcamp

Learn full-stack web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js. Our immersive program includes portfolio projects and job placement assistance.

95% of our graduates secure developer roles within 6 months of graduation.

AI & Machine Learning

Master the fundamentals of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Learn Python, TensorFlow, data analysis, and neural networks through hands-on projects.

Our AI program partners with leading research institutions and tech companies.

Cybersecurity Training

Become a cybersecurity expert with our comprehensive training program. Learn ethical hacking, network security, cryptography, and incident response.

Prepare for industry certifications like CISSP, CEH, and CompTIA Security+.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join TECH-SISTERS?

TECH-SISTERS is open to all women and gender minorities who are interested in technology, regardless of their current skill level or background. We welcome beginners, students, professionals, and anyone looking to transition into tech.

Are your programs free?

We offer both free and paid programs. Our introductory workshops and community events are completely free. Our intensive bootcamps and certification programs have tuition fees, but we provide scholarships and payment plans to ensure accessibility.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required for most of our programs! We offer beginner-friendly courses that start from the basics. For our advanced programs, we provide prerequisite materials to help you get up to speed.

What support do you provide after graduation?

We provide lifelong career support including job placement assistance, interview preparation, resume reviews, networking events, and access to our alumni network of 5,000+ tech professionals. Our graduates also get lifetime access to our learning platform.
