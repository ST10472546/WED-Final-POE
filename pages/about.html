About TECH-SISTERS

Our Mission

TECH-SISTERS was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where women dominate the technology landscape through sisterhood, innovation, and unwavering support. We believe that when women unite in tech, incredible things happen. We make robotics more inclusive, accessible and appealing. We design robots that are not only technologically advanced but also aesthetically pleasing and design philosophy.

Our Story

TECH-SISTERS was found and developed in 2025 by a brilliant Software Developer student named SEDUMA MOHAU, who believes that Technology should be as beautiful as it is functional. TECH-SISTERS emerged from the need to address the gender gap in technology. What started as a small meetup group has grown into a global movement, impacting thousands of women worldwide and creating lasting change in the tech industry.
